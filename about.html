About Us

Bethel Home Services is a Non-governmental Organization that has the thoughts of students at heart.


At B.H.S, we help students get accommodation at cheaper rates with convience.

Getting accommodation for students has always been a problem and also a stressful process.


Lack of accommodation leads to stress to and fro school. It also affects the academic performance of such students.

At Bethel Home, your comfortability is our hope. We want to help you create a conducive environment for living. We also hope it is a place for you to be.
